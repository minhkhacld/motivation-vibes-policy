Privacy Policy for Motivations Vibes

Last Updated: September 02, 2025

Motivations Vibes is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application, Motivations Vibes ("the App"), which provides over 2,000 inspirational quotes from notable individuals, allows users to create custom notifications, and manage widgets on the home screen. The App operates entirely offline, does not require user login, and does not connect to the internet. Below, we outline our practices regarding data collection, use, and storage.

1. No Data Collection

Motivations Vibes does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The App operates completely offline and does not require or request access to personal data such as your name, email address, location, or any other identifiable information. All features, including the quote library, custom notifications, and widget management, function without internet connectivity or external data transmission.

2. Data Storage

All data generated or managed by the App, such as custom notifications or widget settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to, nor do we store, any of this data on external servers or databases. Since the App does not make any API calls or connect to the internet, your data remains entirely under your control on your device.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

As Motivations Vibes does not collect any personal information or connect to external services, we do not share any data with third parties. The App does not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party platforms.

4. Permissions

Motivations Vibes only requests permissions necessary for its core functionality, such as displaying widgets on your home screen or scheduling notifications. These permissions are used solely to enable the App's offline features and do not involve collecting or transmitting any data.

5. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and the App operates offline, the security of your data depends on the security measures you apply to your device. We recommend keeping your device secure with up-to-date software, strong passwords, and other protective measures to safeguard your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Motivations Vibes is designed to be a safe and offline experience suitable for users of all ages. As we do not collect any personal information, there are no specific concerns regarding children's privacy under applicable laws.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted within the App, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
